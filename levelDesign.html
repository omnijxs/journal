What I built

Darken Order: Temple of the Darken Lady

(01/2021): Small/Medium sized intensive cathedral with a new puzzle mechanic which seeks to give more role playing options for Paladins and Clerics good or evil.

Vrage’s Watch

(10/2019): Medium/Large sized fort/crypt with a balanced set of puzzles and action which seeks to give more role playing options for Necromancers.

Lost Spells: Deathchantress

(06/2019): Adds thirteen new and balanced spells to the game seeking to give more role playing options for Conjurers, Necromancers and Frost Mages.

Isle of Storm

(05/2019): Medium/Large sized island and fort with a storm theme which seeks to give fighters a boost against magical foes. Contains lots of multiple paths to finish and plenty of puzzles.

Frostfaarn

(01/2019): Medium/Large sized crypt with multiple routes which seeks to give Necromancers and Conjurers more role playing options.

Snowgaard

(11/2018): Small/Medium sized action heavy crypt which seeks to give Conjurers more role playing options.

Winter’s Grasp

(10/2018) Small/Medium sized linear fort which seeks to give frost magic a boost to make in par with fire and shock magic.
