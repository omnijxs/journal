Snowgaard

(Originally posted on 01.11.2018)

What

  • A mod for Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim
  • A draugr crypt located between Dawnstar and Winterhold, east of Yngvild

Why

  • To give more variance and role playing options for conjurers. Contains three new conjurations spells (Ice Wolf, Snowy Sabre Cat, Snow Bear). These are especially powerful when your character is in lower levels (less than 30)

Design

  • The level is a linear and action heavy experience with a few puzzles
  • It favors melee fighters while being more challenging for sneaky and ranged characters.
  • Gives the player more of a reactive challenge. This means that the action is more forced on the player and your task is to react to what the level throws at you.
  • Lighting and clutter are set to implicitly guide the player through the level

Loot

  • Conjure Ice Wolf Spell: Novice level, default cost 107
  • Conjure Snowy Sabre Cat Spell: Apprentice level, default cost 179
  • Conjure Snow Bear Spell: Adept level, default cost 215
  • Note that only Conjuration perks which affect these spells are Novice Conjuration, Apprentice Conjuration, Adept Conjuration, Expert Conjuration and Master Conjuration.
